Begin the Experience

Concept

The screen is filled with gently moving waves of color.

Waves overlap, resonate, and blend naturally.

There are no objectives.

No timers.

No pressure.

Just something you want to watch… and gently poke.

Interaction

Core gestures:

  • Tap

    Creates a new wave at the point of contact.

  • Swipe

    Changes the direction of existing waves.

  • Long Tap

    Calms a wave, making it slower and wider.

  • Wave Overlap

    Colors blend when waves intersect.

You’ll find yourself guiding the waves that are already there instead of constantly making new ones.

Experience

  • At first, interaction feels playful and exploratory.

  • Over time, players create fewer waves and focus on balance.

  • The experience becomes meditative and reflective.

  • Each time you use it feels totally unique to you.

You can't win at this thing. It's all about what you feel.
